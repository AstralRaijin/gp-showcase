Honda CB500X

The Honda CB500X is a versatile adventure bike designed for both urban commuting and off-road exploration. It features a 471cc parallel-twin engine, comfortable ergonomics, and advanced ABS for safety. Ideal for riders seeking reliability and excitement on varied terrains.

KTM 390 ADV

The KTM 390 Adventure is a compact adventure motorcycle with a 373cc single-cylinder engine, delivering agile performance for city streets and trails. It includes TFT display, adjustable suspension, and cornering ABS, making it perfect for adventurous riders on a budget.
